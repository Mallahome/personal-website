About Me

With over 10 years of experience in youth engagement across the world, I am passionate about creating impactful programs that empower young people. My work has ranged from grassroots initiatives to policy advocacy, always focused on building opportunities and inspiring leadership among youth. Over the years, i have mastered the art of supporting young people dealing with mental health challenges and has enabled my experience to provide clinical care that fosters client satisfaction. it is my desire to see that children, young people and young adults can get the best out of themselves through self involvement in life skills building, participation in community initiatives and being intentional in realizing personal goals.

Experience

  • Program Manager - [Loft community services] (2022 to date)

    Providing strategic leadership towards supporting youths to achieve their clinical treatment through tailored programs addressing barriers related to mental health while providing a safe place of abode.

  • Shelter Diversion Manager - [The good shepherd] (2021-2022)

    Provided leadership in piloting of the emergency housing solutions to Canadians experiencing homelessness due to disaster related to fire, flooding and natural disasters with a quick response team to be able to find alternate housing to the clients..

  • Youth engagement manager - [Plan International Uganda] (2012-2019)

    Led cross-border initiatives aimed at fostering collaboration among youth leaders in East Africa and across the Mediterranean that saw the empowerment and skilling of youths through inspirational incubator hubs. This position provided an opportunity to discover hidden talents and skills within the youth ecosystem that was previously under supported.
